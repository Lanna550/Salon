# Premium Korean Salon Website

## Project Overview

A luxury Korean beauty salon website inspired by boutique salons and premium beauty brands.

The goal is to communicate expertise, elegance, trust, and professionalism.

This project should feel comparable to a real commercial salon website rather than a template.

---

# Brand Positioning

## Market Position

Premium

Not budget.

Not discount focused.

Not mass market.

---

## Target Audience

Primary Audience:

Women aged 22-45.

Interested in:

- Korean beauty
- hair styling
- premium salon experiences
- self-care

Income Level:

Middle to upper-middle class.

---

# Brand Personality

Elegant

Warm

Feminine

Trustworthy

Professional

Sophisticated

Premium

Calm

---

# Emotional Goal

Users should feel:

"I trust these professionals."

"I am willing to pay premium pricing."

# Motion Design Specification

## Philosophy

Motion should feel natural.

Motion should feel expensive.

Motion should never feel playful.

---

# Allowed Animations

Fade In

Duration:

600ms

---

Fade Up

Duration:

700ms

Distance:

20px

---

Image Hover

Scale:

1.02

Duration:

300ms

---

Button Hover

TranslateY(-2px)

Duration:

250ms

---

# Forbidden Animations

Bounce

Shake

Pulse

Spin

Infinite animations

Parallax

Typing effect

Count-up statistics

Flying elements

---

# Photography Direction

Images must be:

- warm
- soft
- premium
- natural

Avoid:

- stock office photos
- AI-looking models
- overprocessed images

---

# Mobile Experience

Mobile experience is equally important.

Content must stack naturally.

No horizontal scrolling.

Buttons must remain thumb-friendly.

Minimum touch target:

44px

---

# Accessibility

Maintain WCAG compliance.

Use semantic HTML.

Ensure contrast ratios are valid.

Provide alt text.

Ensure keyboard navigation.

When users visit the website:
